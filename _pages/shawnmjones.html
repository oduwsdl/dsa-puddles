Shawn M. Jones

Future PhD Graduate

I have worked in industry for more than 18 years, participating in many aspects of systems and software engineering.

I am future employee of the Los Alamos National Laboratory CCS-3 Information Sciences Division. For the moment I am a Graduate Research Assistant at Los Alamos National Laboratory working for the Research Library Prototyping Team. I am also a Ph.D. candidate at Old Dominion University majoring in Computer Science under the guidance of Dr. Michael L. Nelson. My focus area is digital preservation, specifically web archiving.
